# Canal St (ID: 410)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 6  
**Line**: Lexington Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **unknown**

The Canal St station (ID 410, 6 train / Lexington Avenue Line) is listed as ADA
accessible by the MTA. This station is part of a larger Canal Street interchange
complex serving multiple lines including the 4/5/6 (Lexington), N/Q/R/W (Broadway),
and J/Z (Jamaica). The elevator is located on the northeast corner of Canal Street
and Lafayette Street.

The station was likely made accessible under the Key Station Program as a major
interchange in Lower Manhattan/Chinatown. The exact year of the accessibility upgrade
has not been confirmed.
