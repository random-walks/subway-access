# 68 St-Hunter College (ID: 399)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 6
**Line**: Lexington
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

ADA accessibility upgrades completed December 2024 as part of the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program. The project cost $177 million and was completed ahead of schedule and $21 million under budget. This was the ninth subway station made accessible in 2024 and completed the 100 Key Station program. Upgrades included new elevators, new stairways, and wayfinding signage.
