# Jay St-MetroTech (ID: 25)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: A C F R
**Line**: 8th Avenue
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Station ID 25 represents one of the two platform groups at Jay Street-MetroTech
in Downtown Brooklyn. ADA accessibility was achieved through a $164.5 million
renovation completed on December 10, 2010. The project connected two formerly
separate stations (the IND Jay Street station and the BMT Lawrence Street
station) into a single unified complex, installing three ADA-compliant elevators
and two escalators. The upgrade year of 2010 is determined by the documented
renovation completion date.

This station ID shares its ADA upgrade with station ID 174 (A/C/F), both part
of the same Jay Street-MetroTech unification project.
