# 14 St (ID: 229)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: F M
**Line**: 6th Avenue
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

ADA accessibility upgrades completed 2024 as part of the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program. This was part of the 14th Street Complex megaproject. Three elevators were installed connecting the street level to the platform.
