# 59 St (ID: 35)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: N R  
**Line**: 4th Avenue  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

The 59 St station on the BMT Fourth Avenue Line became fully ADA accessible on November 6, 2020, when three new elevators opened. The project included one mezzanine-to-street elevator and two mezzanine-to-platform elevators.

A $55.2 million contract for the elevator installation was awarded in late 2018, with substantial completion originally expected in July 2020. The project was delayed, likely due to COVID-19, and the elevators opened in November 2020. In addition to the elevators, crews expanded the northside mezzanine, built new stairs, and installed a new public address system and fire alarms.
