# Grand Central-42 St (ID: 465)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 7
**Line**: Flushing
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

This station is part of the Grand Central-42nd Street complex, which received major
accessibility upgrades including new elevators as part of the 2015-2019 MTA Capital
Program, with work continuing into 2020. The IRT Flushing Line platform (7 train) is
accessible via the shared elevator infrastructure within the complex.
