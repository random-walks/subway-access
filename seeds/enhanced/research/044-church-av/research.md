# Church Av (ID: 44)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: B Q  
**Line**: Brighton  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Church Av station on the BMT Brighton Line became ADA accessible on November 10, 2025, when new elevators, a customer service center, new staircases, and a new entrance opened. The elevators are located at an entrance on the east side of East 18th Street between Caton and Church Avenues, providing access to both platforms.

The project was part of a $965 million design-build contract awarded in November 2022 to a joint venture of ASTM and Halmar International for the installation of 21 elevators across eight stations. Southbound trains bypassed the station from August 4 to November 23, 2024, and northbound trains skipped the station until February 17, 2025 during construction.

Note: This is a different station from Church Av on the IND Culver Line (F/G, ID 356, accessible since 2008) and from another Church Av B/Q entry (ID 243).
