# 161 St-Yankee Stadium (ID: 219)

**Borough**: Bronx
**Routes**: B D
**Line**: Concourse
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Elevators were installed at the 161 St-Yankee Stadium IND Concourse station in the early 2000s as part of a 3-year renovation project under the 2000-2004 MTA Capital Program. The station opened as fully accessible in late 2002, making it the fourth Bronx station to achieve full ADA compliance. This station forms part of the 161 St-Yankee Stadium complex along with the IRT Jerome Avenue Line platform (station ID 389), which shares the elevator infrastructure.
