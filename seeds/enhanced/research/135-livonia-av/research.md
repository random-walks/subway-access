# Livonia Av (ID: 135)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: L  
**Line**: Canarsie  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Livonia Av station on the BMT Canarsie Line became ADA accessible in 2020 with the opening of two new ADA-compliant elevators. The project was funded through the 2015-2019 MTA Capital Program as one of the accessibility priority projects.

The MTA issued a press release announcing the elevator opening, confirming the station's new fully accessible status.

## Sources

1. MTA press release -- "ADA Accessible Elevators at Livonia Av Station Are Now Open"
2. MTA 2015-2019 Capital Program accessibility project list

## Notes

High confidence. The MTA press release directly confirms the elevator opening. The project was funded under the 2015-2019 Capital Program but completed in 2020, which is common for capital projects that span program periods.
