# 59 St-Columbus Circle (ID: 161)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: A B C D
**Line**: 8th Avenue
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2012**

The 59th Street-Columbus Circle station complex underwent a $125M renovation
completed in 2012, funded under the 2005-2009 MTA Capital Program. The project
included the addition of three elevators, making the station ADA accessible.

The Hearst Corporation funded the addition of the three elevators as part of the
renovation of the adjacent Hearst Tower building. This public-private partnership
delivered accessibility improvements alongside the broader station renovation.
