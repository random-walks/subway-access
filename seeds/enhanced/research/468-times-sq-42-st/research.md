# Times Sq-42 St (ID: 468)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: S (42nd St Shuttle)
**Line**: 42nd St Shuttle
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2022**

This station is part of the Times Square-42nd Street complex, which received a new
$40M ADA-accessible entrance with elevator on May 16, 2022. The 42nd Street Shuttle
platform is accessible via the shared elevator infrastructure within the complex.
