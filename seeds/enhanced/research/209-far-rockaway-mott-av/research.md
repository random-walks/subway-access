# Far Rockaway-Mott Av (ID: 209)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: A  
**Line**: Rockaway  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Far Rockaway-Mott Av station underwent a major renovation that was unveiled on May 11, 2012. The renovation included the installation of elevators from the station house to the platforms, as well as tactile warning strips for visually impaired passengers. This renovation was completed just months before Hurricane Sandy struck in October 2012, and the station survived the storm. The accessibility upgrades made the station fully ADA compliant.
