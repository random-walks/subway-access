Upgrade year: **unknown**

The Inwood-207 St station is listed as ADA accessible by the MTA. As the northern
terminal of the A train on the 8th Avenue Line, the station was a priority for the
Key Station Program. The MTA requested elevator funding for this station as early as
1995.

The exact year the accessibility upgrade was completed has not been confirmed through
available sources.
