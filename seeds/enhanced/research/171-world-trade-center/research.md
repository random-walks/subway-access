# World Trade Center (ID: 171)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: E  
**Line**: 8th Av - Fulton St  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **unknown**

The World Trade Center station (E train) is listed as ADA accessible by the MTA.
This is the southern terminal of the E train on the 8th Avenue Line, located beneath
the World Trade Center complex in Lower Manhattan.

The station was likely made accessible under the Key Station Program. Given its
location within the rebuilt WTC complex, accessibility may have been incorporated
during post-9/11 reconstruction. The exact year of the ADA upgrade has not been
confirmed.
