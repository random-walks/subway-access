# Eastern Pkwy-Brooklyn Museum (ID: 341)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: 2 3  
**Line**: Eastern Parkway  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Eastern Pkwy-Brooklyn Museum station on the IRT Eastern Parkway Line became ADA accessible around 2020. Sources indicate the station was made accessible during the pandemic era, consistent with the MTA's accelerated accessibility program under the 2020-2024 Capital Program.

The station serves as a key transit point for the Brooklyn Museum and Brooklyn Botanic Garden.

## Sources

1. Web search results confirming the station has been ADA accessible since 2020
2. MTA 2020-2024 Capital Program documentation listing accessibility projects

## Notes

Medium-high confidence. Multiple sources place the accessibility upgrade at 2020. The exact completion date within 2020 is not precisely documented, but the year is consistent across sources.
