# Bedford Av (ID: 120)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: L
**Line**: Canarsie
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

The Bedford Av station on the L train became ADA accessible on August 6, 2020, as part
of the 2015-2019 MTA Capital Program. The project installed two new ADA-compliant
elevators: one connecting street level to the mezzanine, and one connecting the mezzanine
to the platform level.
