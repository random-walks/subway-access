# Astoria Blvd (ID: 2)

**Borough**: Queens
**Routes**: N W
**Line**: Astoria
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

The Astoria Blvd station underwent a major renovation completed on July 24, 2020,
funded under the 2015-2019 MTA Capital Program. The project included installation of
four elevators, ADA platform boarding zones, rebuilt walkways, and rebuilt staircases.

The station was closed for approximately 9 months (March through December 2019) for
mezzanine replacement as part of the renovation. Upon completion, Astoria Blvd became
the first N/W station in the Astoria neighborhood to be fully ADA compliant.
