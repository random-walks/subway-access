# Bay Pkwy (ID: 68)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: N  
**Line**: Sea Beach  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Bay Pkwy station was rehabilitated in 2012 and three passenger elevators were added with funding from the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA). The elevators include one from the street (NW corner of 86th Street and Bay Parkway) to the mezzanine and one from the mezzanine to each platform.

Bay Pkwy was designated as the 78th ADA key station in the subway system upon completion. The project included a complete station rehabilitation: new lighting, new platform edges, stair rehabilitation, control area rehabilitation, and new platform windscreens. As a designated landmark station, the rehabilitation was carried out in accordance with State Historic Preservation Office requirements.
