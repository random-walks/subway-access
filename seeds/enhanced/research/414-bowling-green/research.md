# Bowling Green (ID: 414)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 4 5  
**Line**: Lexington Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2007**

The Bowling Green station became ADA accessible when its elevators opened to the
public on July 9, 2007. Located at the southern tip of Manhattan near Battery Park,
the station serves the 4 and 5 express trains on the Lexington Avenue Line.
