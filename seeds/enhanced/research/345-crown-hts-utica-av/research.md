## Research Findings

Crown Hts-Utica Av station on the IRT Eastern Parkway Line is ADA accessible via an elevator on the north side of Eastern Parkway west of Utica Avenue. Two narrow staircases and one elevator connect both platforms to a small upper level mezzanine.

- In April 1993, the New York State Legislature approved $9.6 billion for capital improvements, with funds used to renovate nearly one hundred subway stations including Utica Avenue.
