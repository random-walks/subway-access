# 161 St-Yankee Stadium (ID: 389)

**Borough**: Bronx
**Routes**: 4
**Line**: Jerome
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

The 161 St-Yankee Stadium IRT Jerome Avenue Line platform is part of the same station complex as station ID 219 (IND Concourse). The IRT platform became ADA accessible in 2002 via shared elevator infrastructure installed as part of a 3-year renovation under the 2000-2004 MTA Capital Program. The complex was the fourth Bronx station to achieve full ADA compliance.
