# Borough Hall (ID: 335)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: 2 3  
**Line**: Eastern Parkway  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Borough Hall station (2/3 IRT platforms) became ADA accessible in 1993. According to Wikipedia, workers were installing elevators at the Borough Hall IRT stations by 1992 as part of the MTA's plan to make dozens of key stations accessible to passengers with disabilities. The work was finished by the next year (1993).

The elevator for the 2/3 train platforms is located in front of Brooklyn Borough Hall on Joralemon Street. A single platform elevator shaft connects the upper mezzanine with the New Lots Avenue-bound platform with a stop for the Manhattan-bound platform in between.

This station ID (335) covers only the 2/3 (Broadway-Seventh Avenue Line) platforms. The 4/5 express platforms (ID 415) received separate accessibility upgrades much later, with elevators opening in December 2025 as part of a $106 million project announced in November 2022.
