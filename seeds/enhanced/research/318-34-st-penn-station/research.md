# 34 St-Penn Station (ID: 318)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 1 2 3
**Line**: Broadway-7th Ave
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2023**

This station is part of the 34th Street-Penn Station complex, which received
accessibility upgrades completed in January 2023. The IRT Broadway-7th Avenue Line
platforms (1, 2, 3 trains) are accessible via the shared elevator infrastructure
within the Penn Station complex.
