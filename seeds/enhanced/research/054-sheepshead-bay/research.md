# Sheepshead Bay (ID: 54)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: B Q  
**Line**: Brighton  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Sheepshead Bay station became fully ADA accessible on February 19, 2026, when the MTA unveiled two new elevators connecting street level to both B and Q line platforms. The project also included a new entrance with three turnstiles, two exit gates, and two new stairs, expanding the station's footprint.

Additional improvements included widened sidewalks on both sides of the station entrance, ADA push-button door access to enhance entrances, new lighting, emergency lights, three Help Points, updated fire prevention systems, and a new speaker system. The elevators and critical back-of-house spaces were designed and constructed to be floodproof up to 7 feet, strengthening long-term storm resiliency.
