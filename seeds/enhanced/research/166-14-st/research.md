# 14 St (ID: 166)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: A C E  
**Line**: 8th Av - Fulton St  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2024**

The 14 St (A/C/E) station was made fully accessible in December 2024 as part of the
14 St Complex megaproject. This was a $300 million initiative (including $247 million
in federal funds) under the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program that installed nine new
elevators across the entire 14th Street station complex.

The project encompassed multiple station IDs in the complex: ID 166 (A/C/E), ID 322
(1/2/3), ID 115 (L at 8th Av), and ID 116 (L at 6th Av), making the entire complex
ADA accessible.
