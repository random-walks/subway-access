# 62 St (ID: 63)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: N  
**Line**: Sea Beach  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

The 62 St station is part of the 62nd Street/New Utrecht Avenue station complex, shared by the open-cut BMT Sea Beach Line (N train) and the elevated BMT West End Line (D train). The complex was rehabilitated from 2015 to 2019, with four ADA-accessible elevators entering service on July 19, 2019.

The four elevators enable step-free travel from the N train all the way up to the D train and vice versa, making the entire multi-level complex fully ADA compliant. The transfer between the two lines had been closed during construction. The station is located at New Utrecht Avenue and 62nd Street in Borough Park and Bensonhurst, Brooklyn.
