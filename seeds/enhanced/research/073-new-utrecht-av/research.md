# New Utrecht Av (ID: 73)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: N  
**Line**: Sea Beach  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

New Utrecht Av station is part of the 62nd Street/New Utrecht Avenue station complex, which is shared by the open-cut BMT Sea Beach Line (N train) and the elevated BMT West End Line (D train). The station complex underwent a rehabilitation from 2015 to 2019 that included the installation of four ADA-accessible elevators.

The elevators entered service on July 19, 2019, and enable step-free travel between both the Sea Beach and West End line platforms. The transfer between the two lines had been closed during the construction period.

This station ID (73) represents the N train (Sea Beach Line) portion of the complex at New Utrecht Avenue, while ID 63 represents the same complex from the D/N perspective.
