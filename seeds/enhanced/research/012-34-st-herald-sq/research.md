## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **unknown**

The 34 St-Herald Sq station (N/Q/R/W) is listed as ADA accessible by the MTA. The
station is part of the same complex as ID 227 (B/D/F/M at 34 St-Herald Sq).
Accessibility is provided via an elevator through the Herald Center building.

The station was likely made accessible under the Key Station Program. As a major
Midtown interchange connecting the Broadway and 6th Avenue Lines, it was a high
priority for ADA compliance. The exact year of the upgrade has not been confirmed.
