# 14 St-Union Sq (ID: 15)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: N Q R W  
**Line**: Broadway - Brighton  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **unknown**

The 14 St-Union Sq station (N/Q/R/W) is listed as ADA accessible by the MTA. This
is a major interchange at Union Square connecting the Broadway Line, the Lexington
Avenue Line (4/5/6), and the Canarsie Line (L). The station shares the Union Square
complex with ID 117 (L train).

The station was likely made accessible under the Key Station Program as one of the
busiest interchanges in the system. The exact year of the accessibility upgrade has
not been confirmed.
