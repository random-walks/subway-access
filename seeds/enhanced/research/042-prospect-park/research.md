# Prospect Park (ID: 42)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: B Q S
**Line**: Brighton
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **1996**

The Prospect Park station underwent a $12 million renovation from November 1992 through
1996, funded under the 1992-1999 MTA Capital Program. The project included installation
of ADA-compliant elevators connecting each platform to the station house located on the
north side of the Lincoln Road overpass.
