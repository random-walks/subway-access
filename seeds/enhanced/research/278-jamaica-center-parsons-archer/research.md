# Jamaica Center-Parsons/Archer (ID: 278)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: E J Z  
**Line**: Queens - Archer  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Jamaica Center-Parsons/Archer opened on December 11, 1988 as part of the Archer Avenue Line extension. The station was built new as part of the 1982-1986 MTA Capital Program and was designed with full accessibility from the start, including elevators and accessible platforms. Although the ADA was not enacted until 1990, the Archer Avenue Line stations were constructed with accessibility features incorporated into the original design. All three Archer Avenue stations (Jamaica Center-Parsons/Archer, Sutphin Blvd-Archer Av-JFK Airport, and Jamaica-Van Wyck) opened on the same day.
