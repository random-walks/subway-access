### Regression discontinuity (800 m walk-radius cutoff)

This is a **specification check**: for uncovered tracts, `gap_score` is defined as `need_score`, and for covered tracts, `gap_score = 0`. The 800 m catchment drives the covered/uncovered boundary, so an RDD at 800 m with `gap_score` as the outcome should detect a mechanical discontinuity of approximately `-mean(need_score)` when crossing from uncovered to covered. A null estimate would signal either catchment-radius drift or a data issue.
