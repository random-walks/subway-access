# Arthur Kill (ID: 523)

**Borough**: Staten Island  
**Routes**: SIR  
**Line**: Staten Island  
**Division**: SIR

## Research Findings

Arthur Kill station opened on January 21, 2017 as the newest station on the Staten Island Railway. The station was built with ADA accessibility from the start, featuring accessible ramps on both sides of the overpass. As a brand-new station, it was designed and constructed to meet all current ADA requirements without the need for retrofit upgrades.
