# Times Sq-42 St (ID: 11)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: N Q R W
**Line**: Broadway
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2022**

A new $40M ADA-accessible entrance with elevator opened on May 16, 2022, at the
Times Square-42nd Street station complex. The project was part of a private-public
partnership with Jamestown L.P. as part of the One Times Square redevelopment. This
added the sixth ADA elevator serving the station complex.

The project was funded under the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program and delivered a new
street-level accessible entrance connecting to the BMT Broadway platforms (N, Q, R,
W trains).
