# Bleecker St (ID: 408)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 6  
**Line**: Lexington Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2012**

The Bleecker St station became ADA accessible on September 25, 2012, when a free
transfer with elevators was opened connecting the uptown IRT platform to the IND
Broadway-Lafayette St station (ID 230). This was part of a $50 million renovation
project announced in 2005 under the 2005-2009 MTA Capital Program.

The project created an accessible connection between the Lexington Avenue Line (6
train) and the 6th Avenue Line (B/D/F/M trains), significantly improving transit
options for riders with disabilities in the NoHo/SoHo area.
