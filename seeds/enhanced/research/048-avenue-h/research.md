# Avenue H (ID: 48)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: B Q  
**Line**: Brighton  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Avenue H station became fully ADA accessible in June 2021 with the completion of a ramp to the northbound platform. This was notably the first completed ADA project from the 2020-2024 Capital Plan.

The station uses ramps rather than elevators for accessibility, appropriate for this ground-level, open-cut station. The landmarked 1906 wooden station house made elevator installation impractical, so ramps were the chosen solution. The southbound ramp had been completed earlier, in September 2011, but full ADA compliance required both platforms to be accessible.

The total project cost was $18 million, including $12.2 million for construction.
