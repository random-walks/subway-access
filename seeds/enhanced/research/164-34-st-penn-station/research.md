# 34 St-Penn Station (ID: 164)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: A C E
**Line**: 8th Avenue
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2023**

The 34th Street-Penn Station complex on the 8th Avenue Line received accessibility
upgrades completed in January 2023. The project included four elevator replacements
and a new accessible entrance at 33rd Street and 7th Avenue, funded under the
2020-2024 MTA Capital Program.
