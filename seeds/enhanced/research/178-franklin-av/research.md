# Franklin Av (ID: 178)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: A C  
**Line**: Fulton  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Franklin Av station on the IND Fulton Street Line became ADA accessible as part of the 1998-1999 reconstruction of the Franklin Avenue Shuttle. The station complex, which provides an in-system transfer between the A/C trains and the Franklin Avenue Shuttle, was renovated with full elevator access to the platforms.

Two elevators were installed: one from the shuttle platform going down to the overpass, main fare control area, and the southbound IND platform. An enclosed transfer was built with the elevators and an escalator to provide better access between the Franklin Avenue Shuttle and the IND Fulton Street Line. These elevators make the entire station complex ADA-accessible.

The station reopened on October 18, 1999. This station ID (178) represents the A/C (IND Fulton Street Line) portion of the complex; the shuttle portion is tracked under ID 139.
