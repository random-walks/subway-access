# Church Av (ID: 356)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: F G

## Research Findings

Church Av station on the IND Culver Line became fully ADA accessible on July 7, 2008, when three elevators opened. The station is located at Church and McDonald Avenues in Kensington, Brooklyn, and is served by the F train at all times and the G train (which terminates here) at all times.

Note: There is a data discrepancy. The scaffolded research file lists this station as 2/5 on the Nostrand/IRT line. The user's instructions identify it as F/G on the Culver/IND line. This research covers the IND Culver Line station per the user's specifications. For reference, the IRT Nostrand Avenue Line Church Av (2/5) was separately made accessible in Spring 1999 with two elevators.

There are three different Church Avenue stations in the subway system:
- Church Av F/G (IND Culver Line, this station ID 356) -- accessible since 2008
- Church Av B/Q (BMT Brighton Line, ID 44) -- accessible since November 2025
- Church Av 2/5 (IRT Nostrand Avenue Line) -- accessible since Spring 1999
