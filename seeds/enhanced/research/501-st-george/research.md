# St George (ID: 501)

**Borough**: Staten Island  
**Routes**: SIR  
**Line**: Staten Island  
**Division**: SIR

## Research Findings

St George is the northern terminal of the Staten Island Railway and is ADA accessible. The station features elevators connecting the train level, main floor, and street level, as well as ramps. It is a major transportation hub adjacent to the St. George Ferry Terminal, which provides Staten Island Ferry service to Manhattan. The exact year when ADA accessibility upgrades were completed is unclear from available sources.
