# 8 Av (ID: 115)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: L  
**Line**: Canarsie  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2024**

The 8 Av (L train) station at 14th Street was made fully accessible in December 2024
as part of the 14 St Complex megaproject. This is the L train platform at the 14 St
and 8th Avenue intersection.

The station shares the complex with ID 166 (A/C/E), ID 322 (1/2/3), and ID 116 (L
at 6th Av). The $300 million project installed nine new elevators across the entire
complex under the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program.
