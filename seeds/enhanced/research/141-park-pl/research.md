# Park Pl (ID: 141)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: S (Franklin Avenue Shuttle)  
**Line**: Franklin Avenue  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Park Pl station on the BMT Franklin Avenue Line was rebuilt as part of the 1998-1999 reconstruction of the Franklin Avenue Shuttle. The rebuilt station, which consists of a single side platform and a single track, includes a long ADA-accessible ramp and staircase going to the south side of Prospect Place on the north side of the station house.

The station uses a ramp rather than an elevator for accessibility, suitable for its configuration. The ramp is located at Prospect Place west of Franklin Avenue, providing service in both directions on the single track.

The reconstruction was part of the $74 million project that rebuilt the entire Franklin Avenue Shuttle line, which was replaced by buses for over a year during the work.
