# Greenpoint Av (ID: 283)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: G  
**Line**: Crosstown  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Greenpoint Av station on the IND Crosstown Line became fully ADA accessible in late November 2020 with the opening of three new elevators. The project was part of the 2015-2019 MTA Capital Program.

The three elevators include one from the mezzanine to street level on the eastern side of Manhattan Avenue north of Greenpoint Avenue, and one to each platform. Construction began in September 2018 and took 29 months to complete, with the elevators opening a few days ahead of the expected December 2020 target.

The project cost $23.4 million. Greenpoint Av was a significant milestone as only the second elevator ever installed on the G line.
