# Franklin Av (ID: 139)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: S (Franklin Avenue Shuttle)  
**Line**: Franklin Avenue  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Franklin Av shuttle station was completely rebuilt between 1998 and 1999 as part of a $74 million reconstruction of the entire Franklin Avenue Shuttle line. The rebuilt station reopened on October 18, 1999, three months ahead of schedule.

Two elevators were installed as part of the rebuild. One elevator goes from the shuttle platform down to the overpass, main fare control area, and the southbound IND platform. These two elevators make the entire Franklin Avenue station complex (connecting the shuttle with the A/C IND Fulton Street Line) ADA-accessible.

An enclosed transfer was built with two elevators and an escalator to provide better access between the Franklin Avenue Shuttle and the IND Fulton Street Line.
