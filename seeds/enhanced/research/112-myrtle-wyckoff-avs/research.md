# Myrtle-Wyckoff Avs (ID: 112)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: L  
**Line**: Canarsie  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Myrtle-Wyckoff Avenues station became ADA accessible in fall 2007 when three new elevators were put into service. The station complex was renovated from 2004 to 2008, with the main station building formally opening on April 19, 2007. The elevator installation, which enabled full ADA compliance, occurred several months later that same year.

The three elevators provide a complete accessible path through the multi-level complex: one from the Canarsie Line (L train) platform to the lower mezzanine, one from the lower mezzanine to street level and the upper mezzanine, and one from the upper mezzanine to the Myrtle Avenue Line (M train) platform.

This station ID (112) represents the L train (Canarsie Line) portion of the complex. The M train (Myrtle Avenue Line) portion is tracked under ID 128.
