# 86 St (ID: 38)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: R
**Line**: 4th Avenue
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

The 86 St station on the R train became ADA accessible in August 2020, funded under the
2015-2019 MTA Capital Program. The project installed elevators to provide step-free
access. The station was made accessible around the same time as the Bedford Av L station
(ID 120), which opened its ADA elevators on August 6, 2020.
