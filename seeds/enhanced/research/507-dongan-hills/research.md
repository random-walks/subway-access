# Dongan Hills (ID: 507)

**Borough**: Staten Island  
**Routes**: SIR  
**Line**: Staten Island  
**Division**: SIR

## Research Findings

Dongan Hills station was made ADA accessible in 1993, making it one of the earliest Staten Island Railway stations to receive accessibility upgrades. The station uses ramps rather than elevators for ADA compliance, which is common for SIR stations given their relatively low platform heights and at-grade or near-grade configurations. The accessibility work was completed shortly after the ADA was enacted in 1990.
