# Great Kills (ID: 513)

**Borough**: Staten Island  
**Routes**: SIR  
**Line**: Staten Island  
**Division**: SIR

## Research Findings

Great Kills station became ADA accessible in 1997 with the construction of an accessible entrance featuring ramps. Like other SIR stations, Great Kills uses ramps rather than elevators for ADA compliance, which is feasible given the station's relatively low platform configuration.
