# Times Sq-42 St (ID: 467)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 7
**Line**: Flushing
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2022**

This station is part of the Times Square-42nd Street complex, which received a new
$40M ADA-accessible entrance with elevator on May 16, 2022. The IRT Flushing Line
platform (7 train) is accessible via the shared elevator infrastructure within the
complex.
