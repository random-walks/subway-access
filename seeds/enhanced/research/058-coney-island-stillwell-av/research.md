# Coney Island-Stillwell Av (ID: 58)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: D F N Q
**Line**: Multiple (Sea Beach / West End / Culver / Brighton)
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2004**

The Coney Island-Stillwell Avenue station underwent a complete reconstruction from
2001 to 2004, funded under the 2000-2004 MTA Capital Program. The rebuilt station
reopened on May 23, 2004, featuring ADA-compliant ramps from platforms and elevator
access.

The reconstruction was one of the largest station projects of its era, transforming
the terminal station serving four subway lines (D, F, N, Q). Post-opening, elevator
reliability issues were noted.
