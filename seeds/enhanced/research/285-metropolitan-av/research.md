# Metropolitan Av (ID: 285)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: G L
**Line**: Crosstown/Canarsie

## Research Findings

ADA accessibility upgrades completed 2024 as part of the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program. Six new elevators were installed across the Metropolitan Av and Lorimer St station complex, making both stations fully accessible.
