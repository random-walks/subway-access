# Flushing Av (ID: 98)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: M J  
**Line**: Jamaica  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Station ID 98 is the **BMT Jamaica Line (J/M) Flushing Av station** — an elevated station at Flushing Ave and Broadway in Brooklyn. This was confirmed from the MTA station catalog data which lists station 98 as routes "M J", line "Jamaica", division "BMT", structure "Elevated".

There is a separate Flushing Avenue station on the IND Crosstown Line (G train) which is NOT ADA accessible. That is a different station with a different station ID.

The J/M Flushing Av station was made ADA accessible in fall 2003 with the installation of three elevators, prioritized due to its proximity to Woodhull Hospital.
