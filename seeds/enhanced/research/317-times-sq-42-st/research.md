# Times Sq-42 St (ID: 317)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 1 2 3
**Line**: Broadway-7th Ave
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2022**

This station is part of the Times Square-42nd Street complex, which received a new
$40M ADA-accessible entrance with elevator on May 16, 2022. The IRT Broadway-7th
Avenue platforms (1, 2, 3 trains) are accessible via the shared elevator
infrastructure within the complex.
