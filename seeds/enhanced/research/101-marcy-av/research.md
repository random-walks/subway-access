# Marcy Av (ID: 101)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: J M Z  
**Line**: Jamaica  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Marcy Av station on the BMT Jamaica Line has a complex accessibility history. In 2002, the MTA announced that elevators would be installed at the station. The exact completion date of the original elevator installation is not documented in available sources.

In December 2021, the MTA awarded a contract for the replacement of the station's elevators. The new replacement elevators opened on September 3, 2024, with the station achieving full ADA compliance at that point. The elevators are located on the northwest and southwest corners of Broadway and Marcy Avenue, with the Queens-bound elevator on the southwest corner and the Manhattan-bound elevator on the northwest corner.

Since the original installation date from the early 2000s is undocumented, 2024 is used as the upgrade year, reflecting when the station definitively achieved full ADA compliance with the new elevator installation.
