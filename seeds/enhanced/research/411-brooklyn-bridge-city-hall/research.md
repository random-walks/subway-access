# Brooklyn Bridge-City Hall (ID: 411)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 4 5 6  
**Line**: Lexington Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

The Brooklyn Bridge-City Hall station completed an elevator renovation in September
2020. The station originally had elevators installed in 1992, and the 2020 project
modernized the elevator infrastructure to current ADA standards.

The station is a major interchange serving the 4, 5, and 6 trains on the Lexington
Avenue Line, located near City Hall and the Brooklyn Bridge.
