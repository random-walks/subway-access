# Euclid Av (ID: 188)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: A C  
**Line**: Fulton  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Euclid Av station on the IND Fulton Street Line became ADA accessible by March 2006 following a delayed elevator installation project. In July 2002, the MTA announced the project and awarded a $6.9 million contract for three elevators to Gibraltar Contracting.

The three elevators include one to the street (at the NE corner of Euclid Avenue and Pitkin Avenue) and two between the mezzanine and each platform. The project's original completion date of April 2005 was pushed back twice due to delays, and was over six months behind schedule as of October 2005. The total project cost increased from $14.1 million to $14.3 million. The project was completed by March 2006.

The elevators have been recently modernized. The station is an express station serving the A train at all times and is the southern terminal for the C train at all times except nights.
