# Flushing-Main St (ID: 447)

**Borough**: Queens
**Routes**: 7
**Line**: Flushing
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2000**

The Flushing-Main St terminal station received ADA accessibility as part of a major
renovation completed between 1999 and 2000, funded under the 1995-1999 MTA Capital
Program. The project added an elevator near the Lippmann Plaza exit, along with
escalators to improve vertical circulation within the station.
