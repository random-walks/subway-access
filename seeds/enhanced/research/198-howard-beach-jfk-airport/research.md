## Research Findings

Howard Beach-JFK Airport station is ADA accessible and serves as a major transfer point to the AirTrain JFK, which provides service to John F. Kennedy International Airport terminals. The station is confirmed to be ADA accessible, but the exact year when accessibility upgrades were completed is unclear from available sources. The AirTrain JFK connection opened in December 2003, and accessibility improvements may have been made in conjunction with that project or separately.
