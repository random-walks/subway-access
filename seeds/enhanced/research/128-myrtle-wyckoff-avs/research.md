# Myrtle-Wyckoff Avs (ID: 128)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: M  
**Line**: Myrtle Avenue  
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Myrtle-Wyckoff Avenues station became ADA accessible in fall 2007 when three new elevators were put into service. This station ID (128) represents the M train (Myrtle Avenue Line) platform of the same physical complex tracked under ID 112 for the L train.

The station complex was renovated from 2004 to 2008, with the main station building formally opening on April 19, 2007. The three elevators provide a complete accessible path through the multi-level complex: one from the L train platform to the lower mezzanine, one from the lower mezzanine to street level and the upper mezzanine, and one from the upper mezzanine to the M train platform.

The elevator in the station house is on the south side of Wyckoff Avenue between Gates Avenue and Myrtle Avenue.
