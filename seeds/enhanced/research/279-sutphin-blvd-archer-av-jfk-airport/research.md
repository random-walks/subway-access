# Sutphin Blvd-Archer Av-JFK Airport (ID: 279)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: E J Z  
**Line**: Queens - Archer  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Sutphin Blvd-Archer Av-JFK Airport opened on December 11, 1988 as part of the Archer Avenue Line extension. The station was built new as part of the 1982-1986 MTA Capital Program and was designed with full accessibility from the start, including elevators and accessible platforms. Although the ADA was not enacted until 1990, the Archer Avenue Line stations were constructed with accessibility features incorporated into the original design. This station serves as a transfer point for the AirTrain JFK connection.
