# 66 St-Lincoln Center (ID: 314)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 1  
**Line**: Broadway - 7Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2025**

The 66 St-Lincoln Center station received two modernized elevators that opened in
2025, making the station ADA accessible. The project was funded by the Federal
Transit Administration (FTA) under the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program.

The station serves the 1 train on the Broadway-7th Avenue Line, located adjacent to
Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts on the Upper West Side.
