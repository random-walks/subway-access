# Grand Central-42 St (ID: 402)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 4 5 6
**Line**: Lexington
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2020**

Grand Central-42nd Street received major accessibility upgrades including new
elevators as part of the 2015-2019 MTA Capital Program, with work continuing into
2020. The project was completed on time and $46.5M under budget.

The improvements brought ADA accessibility to the IRT Lexington Avenue Line
platforms (4, 5, 6 trains) at one of the busiest stations in the NYC subway system.
