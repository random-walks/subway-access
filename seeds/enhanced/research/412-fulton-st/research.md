# Fulton St (ID: 412)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 4 5
**Line**: Lexington
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Station ID 412 represents the 4/5 platform of the Fulton Street complex in
Lower Manhattan. ADA accessibility was achieved through the Fulton Center
project, a $1.4 billion transit hub that opened in November 2014. The project
installed 15 elevators and 10 escalators across the complex, connecting five
previously separate stations serving nine subway lines (2, 3, 4, 5, A, C, J, Z,
and the Brooklyn-bound connection to the R and W at Cortlandt St). The upgrade
year of 2014 is determined by the Fulton Center's public opening date.

This station ID shares its ADA upgrade with station IDs 106 (J/Z), 172 (A/C),
and 332 (2/3), all part of the same Fulton Center complex renovation.
