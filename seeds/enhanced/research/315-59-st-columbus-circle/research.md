# 59 St-Columbus Circle (ID: 315)

**Borough**: Manhattan
**Routes**: 1
**Line**: Broadway-7th Ave
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2012**

This station is part of the 59th Street-Columbus Circle complex, which underwent a
$125M renovation completed in 2012. The IRT Broadway-7th Avenue Line platform
(1 train) is accessible via the shared elevator infrastructure installed as part of
the complex renovation.
