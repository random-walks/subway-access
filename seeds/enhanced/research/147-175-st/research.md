# 175 St (ID: 147)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: A  
**Line**: 8th Av - Fulton St  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **unknown**

The 175 St station is listed as ADA accessible by the MTA. This is one of the
deepest stations in the NYC subway system, located in Washington Heights on the 8th
Avenue Line. The elevator is situated on the northeast corner of 177th Street and
Fort Washington Avenue.

The station was likely made accessible under the Key Station Program. The exact year
of the accessibility upgrade has not been confirmed.
