# New Dorp (ID: 510)

**Borough**: Staten Island  
**Routes**: SIR  
**Line**: Staten Island  
**Division**: SIR

## Research Findings

New Dorp station became ADA accessible with the opening of new elevators on September 19, 2023, as part of the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program. This was a milestone for the Staten Island Railway, as New Dorp became the first SIR station to feature elevators for ADA accessibility. Previously, SIR stations relied on ramps for accessibility compliance.
