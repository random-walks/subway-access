# Aqueduct Racetrack (ID: 196)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: A  
**Line**: Rockaway  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Aqueduct Racetrack station was renovated and reopened on August 13, 2013 in connection with the Resorts World Casino (now Resorts World New York City), which opened at the Aqueduct Racetrack site in 2011. The renovation included the addition of an elevator providing access from street level to the platform, making the station ADA accessible. The station had previously been closed and was rebuilt to serve casino patrons and the surrounding community.
