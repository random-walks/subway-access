# Flatbush Av-Brooklyn College (ID: 359)

**Borough**: Brooklyn  
**Routes**: 2 5  
**Line**: Nostrand  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Flatbush Av-Brooklyn College is the terminal station of the IRT Nostrand Avenue Line, serving the 2 and 5 trains. The station became ADA accessible in 1997 when an elevator was installed as part of the MTA's ADA key station compliance program.

The original 1997 elevator was replaced with a modernized unit in 2021. The station is located at the intersection of Flatbush Avenue and Nostrand Avenue near Brooklyn College.
