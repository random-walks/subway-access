# 14 St (ID: 322)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: 1 2 3  
**Line**: Broadway - 7Av  
**Division**: IRT

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2024**

The 14 St (1/2/3) station was made fully accessible in December 2024 as part of the
14 St Complex megaproject. This station shares the complex with ID 166 (A/C/E),
ID 115 (L at 8th Av), and ID 116 (L at 6th Av).

The $300 million project (including $247 million in federal funds) installed nine new
elevators across the entire complex under the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program.
