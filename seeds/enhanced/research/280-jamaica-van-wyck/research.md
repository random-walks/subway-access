# Jamaica-Van Wyck (ID: 280)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: E  
**Line**: Queens - Archer  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Jamaica-Van Wyck opened on December 11, 1988 as part of the Archer Avenue Line extension. The station was built new as part of the 1982-1986 MTA Capital Program and was designed with full accessibility from the start, including elevators and accessible platforms. Although the ADA was not enacted until 1990, the Archer Avenue Line stations were constructed with accessibility features incorporated into the original design. This station is served only by the E train, unlike the other two Archer Avenue stations which also serve the J and Z.
