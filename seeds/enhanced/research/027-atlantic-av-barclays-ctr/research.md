# Atlantic Av-Barclays Ctr (ID: 27)

**Borough**: Brooklyn
**Routes**: B D N Q R
**Line**: 4th Avenue
**Division**: BMT

## Research Findings

Station ID 27 represents the B/D/N/Q/R platform group at the Atlantic
Avenue-Barclays Center complex in Downtown Brooklyn. ADA accessibility was
achieved through a major station overhaul completed in 2012, coinciding with the
opening of the Barclays Center arena. The project installed eight elevators at a
cost of $77.9 million, making the full complex ADA-accessible. The upgrade year
of 2012 is determined by the documented completion of the accessibility
renovation tied to the arena opening.

This station ID shares its ADA upgrade with station IDs 40 (B/Q Brighton line)
and 338 (2/3/4/5 IRT), all part of the same Atlantic Avenue-Barclays Center
complex renovation.
