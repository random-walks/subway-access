# Beach 67 St (ID: 204)

**Borough**: Queens  
**Routes**: A  
**Line**: Rockaway  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Beach 67 St station became fully ADA accessible in January 2024. Two new elevators were installed: one from street level to the mezzanine, and one from the mezzanine to the platform. The project also included a new staircase, ADA-compliant platform edges, fire alarms, smoke and heat detectors, and security cameras in the elevator cabs. The elevators were finished on January 11, 2024, and the station was formally dedicated on January 19, 2024. The project was funded by the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) as part of the 2020-2024 MTA Capital Program.
