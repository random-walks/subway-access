# Broadway-Lafayette St (ID: 230)

**Borough**: Manhattan  
**Routes**: B D F M  
**Line**: 6th Av - Culver  
**Division**: IND

## Research Findings

Upgrade year: **2012**

Broadway-Lafayette St is part of the same station complex as Bleecker St (ID 408).
The station became ADA accessible when elevators connecting the two stations opened
on September 25, 2012. This was part of a $50 million renovation project announced
in 2005 under the 2005-2009 MTA Capital Program.

The elevator connection created a free transfer between the IND 6th Avenue Line
(B/D/F/M) and the IRT Lexington Avenue Line (6), making the entire complex
accessible.
